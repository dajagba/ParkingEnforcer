The Project

The Enforcer App was devised and created by a team of students from Kennesaw State University for the Spring 2018 Senior Project Course. The intent and purpose of the app is to provide university parking staff with rapid information and robust tools. These tools help with the difficult task of managing access to university parking areas.  

The app accomplishes these goals by leveraging smart design and cutting-edge image recognition technology. A small and affordable computer with an internet-enabled camera is used to scan the entrance of a parking lot. When a license plate is recognized, it is verified against a database of registered vehicles for that lot. If it is not registered to park there, it is flagged as invalid and the enforcer dashboard is updated. This process is nearly instantaneous and can be used to provide up-to-the-minute campus parking information.
